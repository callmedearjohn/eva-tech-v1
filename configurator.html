    Custom EVA car mats are durable, convenient and versatile accessories that protect your interior and trunk from dust, dirt and moisture all year round.

    EVA mats are perfect for all-season use. They do not absorb water and remain elastic in cold weather, unlike textile or classic rubber mats.

    EVA mats are made from an eco‑friendly material with high wear resistance, resistance to extreme temperatures (from +50°C to −50°C) and aggressive chemicals. They are lightweight, and the dense structure provides excellent sound insulation.

    >

    The surface has a special rhombus pattern that effectively localizes dirt and water, keeping footwear and interior surfaces clean. The mats are easy to rinse without special tools, including with a touchless wash.

    Advantages of EVA mats

    - The cells reliably hold water. To pour it out, hold the mat almost vertically. This prevents water from splashing like with rubber mats.

    - Eco-friendly and safe material. Odorless and hypoallergenic.

    - All-season use. Remains elastic at −50 / +50.

    - Choice of mat and edging colors.

    - Maximum floor coverage.

    - Reliable fastening to OEM clips plus extra hook-and-loop to the floor.

    - Easy to clean with a stream of water — dirt rinses out from the cells.

    - We guarantee high product quality.

    Popular questions about EVA mats

    • What are polymer car mats made of?
      We use Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) with a rhombus pattern. It is very light yet strong. Advantages: holds a lot of liquid, odorless, resistant to chemicals, safe for health and the environment, and retains properties in heat up to +50°C and cold down to −40°C.
    • What colors are available?
      A wide palette of mat and edging colors is available to get many combinations.
    • What is the thickness and cell depth?
      Material thickness is 10–11 mm, cell depth 5–7 mm.
    • Do the mats absorb water?
      This material does not absorb water.
    • How to care for them?
      Rinse with a stream of water under pressure; accumulated dirt washes out quickly. They are resistant to car shampoos and detergents.
    • How are mats attached to the floor?
      All EVA mats include hook-and-loop for reliable fixation. If OEM mounts are present, the mats include compatible clips.
    • Why are there no sidewalls?
      The rhombus pattern keeps water localized, distributing it evenly without spilling.
    • How do they perform in winter?
      They remain elastic down to −50°C, do not get dirty quickly, and do not emit unpleasant odors.
